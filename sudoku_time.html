Timeless
Classic Sudoku

Classic Sudoku is a timeless logic puzzle that has stood the test of time. Played on a 9×9 grid, you must fill in the empty cells following the core rules: every row, every column, and every 3×3 subgrid must contain the numbers 1 to 9 without repetition.

There are no age limits or time pressure, and no complicated calculations are needed—only logic and focus will lead you to the one and only solution. It sharpens your mind, relieves stress, and brings you pure solving joy in a compact grid. A truly enduring brain-training classic you can enjoy anytime, anywhere.
